Section 01

Executive Summary

A consolidated overview of findings from six integrated discipline studies for the Greensville Estates Development.

The Greensville Estates Development represents a 5.0-hectare estate residential severance at 190 Highway 8 in the Greensville Rural Settlement Area of Hamilton. This comprehensive master report consolidates findings from six integrated discipline studies: Planning, Archaeology, Environmental Assessment, Hydrogeology, Civil Engineering, and Regulatory Coordination.

Project Snapshot

The Application for Consent was submitted December 23, 2025. The project is currently in the Conditional Approval Phase with final regulatory submissions pending. All currently feasible field work is complete. Next milestones are MCM concurrence (Stage 3 then Stage 4) and completion of Phase 2 hydrogeological drilling and testing.

Section 05

Archaeology

Cultural heritage assessment including Stage 3 and Stage 4 archaeological work across three identified areas.

Stage 3 Assessment

The Stage 3 Archaeological Assessment has been submitted to the Ministry of Citizenship and Multiculturalism (MCM) and is currently under review pending concurrence. The assessment identified three archaeological areas requiring further investigation.

Stage 4 Assessment

Stage 4 field work has been completed across all three archaeological areas. The approach utilized a combination of:

  • Avoidance and long-term protection for culturally significant features
  • Mitigation excavation for areas that cannot be avoided by development

The Stage 4 report is currently in progress and will be submitted following Stage 3 MCM concurrence.

Section 07

Hydrogeology

Groundwater assessment, well drilling program, and private servicing feasibility for the 5-lot development.

Phase 2 Drilling Program

Phase 2 hydrogeological drilling is scheduled to begin February 22, 2026. This phase involves drilling 2 additional bedrock wells to complete the site's water servicing infrastructure.

Stepwise Drilling Program

The program is designed stepwise to manage risk and optimize results:

Step 1: Drill one well and monitor immediately for 2 to 3 days.
Step 2: Confirm results from the first well.
Step 3: Complete the second well.

Upon Completion

When Phase 2 is complete, the site will have a comprehensive groundwater monitoring network:

3
Full-Service Wells
4
Monitoring Wells

All well data will be submitted for City of Hamilton review as part of the private servicing approval process.
