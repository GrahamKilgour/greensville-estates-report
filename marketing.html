The Vision

A Rare Opportunity in Greensville

Greensville Estates offers a once-in-a-generation opportunity to build your dream home on an estate lot within one of Hamilton's most prestigious and established rural communities — surrounded by nature, steeped in heritage, and minutes from everything.

Why Greensville

Nestled at the edge of the Niagara Escarpment, Greensville is a historic village that has maintained its character while being just minutes from Dundas, Hamilton, and the 403. The area is known for its mature tree canopy, heritage homes, and proximity to some of Ontario's finest conservation lands.

These five estate lots represent one of the last opportunities for new estate-scale development in the Greensville Rural Settlement Area — a community with extremely limited supply and enduring demand.
